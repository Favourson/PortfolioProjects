Frequently asked Questions

Covenant University FAQ

What is Covenant University?

Covenant University is a world-class institution committed to academic excellence, leadership development, and positive societal impact.

Where is Covenant University located?

Covenant University is located in Lagos, Nigeria, and also has a branch in London, and runs its Online University as well.

What programs does Covenant University offer?

Covenant University offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs in various fields of study, including engineering, science, social sciences, management, and the arts.

What are the admission requirements for Covenant?

Admission requirements vary depending on the program of study. Generally, applicants are required to have completed secondary school education and meet certain academic and English language proficiency requirements.

How do I apply to Covenant University?

Prospective students can apply to Covenant University online through the university's website or in person at the admissions office.

What is the tuition fee for Covenant University?

Tuition fees vary depending on the program of study. Prospective students are encouraged to visit the university's website or contact the admissions office for more information.

Does Covenant University offer scholarships?

Yes, Covenant University offers scholarships to eligible students based on academic merit and financial need.

What is the student-faculty ratio at Covenant University?

The student-faculty ratio at Covenant University is 19:1, which ensures personalized attention and support from faculty members.

Does Covenant University have international students?

Yes, Covenant University welcomes international students from all over the world.

What kind of facilities does Covenant University offer?

Covenant University offers state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and student accommodation.

Does Covenant University have a student support system?

Yes, Covenant University offers a range of student support services, including academic advising, counseling, career services, and student clubs and organizations.

What kind of research does Covenant University conduct?

Covenant University is committed to promoting research and innovation in various fields of study, including agriculture, engineering, health, and the social sciences.

Does Covenant University have partnerships with other institutions?

Yes, Covenant University has partnerships with several universities and research institutions around the world, which offer opportunities for international collaboration and exchange programs.

What is the career prospects for Covenant University graduates?

Covenant University graduates are highly sought after by employers in various industries, and many have gone on to successful careers in academia, research, business, and government.

What is the mission of Covenant University?

The mission of Covenant University is to raise a new generation of leaders who are capable of transforming their communities and the world through academic excellence, moral and ethical values, and innovation.
